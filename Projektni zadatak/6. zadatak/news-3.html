Debonair 2021

Debonair 2021 is last event in 2021 with a lot of rewards, new skins, pass and pass bundle.

Debonair event starts on 09 December 2021 and will continue until 10 January 2022. Missions will unlock with start of the event and will end at the end of event but you can still spend tokens(won in the missions) until 17 January 2022.

New event will bring us 8 new skins for champions: Zed, Draven, Leona, LeBlanc, Malzahar, Master Yi and Brand. You can purches skins in shop from 1350 RP to 1820 RP (for Debonair Zed - legendary skin).

The Debonair 2021 Event Pass (1650 RP) and Debonair 2021 Event Pass Bundle (2650 RP) will be available for purchase beginning 09 December 2021. The standard Debonair 2021 Event Pass includes your Event Pass, 4 Debonair 2021 Orbs, and 200 Debonair 2021 Tokens. The Debonair 2021 Event Pass Bundle contains all the items mentioned above, as well as the Debonair Brand Icon, Debonair Brand Skin, and Brand Champion.

How to get points for missions? It is easy. You just need to play games. If you win you will get extra points and you can faster get rewards. In total you have 10 missions to complete if you want all free rewards.

We prepared so much more for you. In our shop you can purchase new chromas, ward skins, border sets and emotes. Enjoy in Debonair event!

Source: support-leagueoflegends.riotgames.com
