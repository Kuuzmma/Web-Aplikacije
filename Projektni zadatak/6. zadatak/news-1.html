New champion Vex

Vex was the third of three champions released in 2021

Vex primari role is mage and not difficulte to play with her. She have like other passive and 4 abilities.

At first, lets talk about passive Vex periodically becomes empowered, causing her next basic Ability to fear enemies and interrupt dashes.

On Q MISTRAL BOLT launch a damaging missile that accelerates mid-flight. On W PERSONAL SPACE she gain a shield and damage nearby enemies. On E LOOMING DARKNESS summon a damaging and slowing zone that applies Gloom to enemies.

On R like other champions she got ulty, her is SHADOW SURGE and she fire a missile that marks an enemy champion. Recast to dash to them and deal damage.

Vex come with one epic skin Dawnbringer Vex and can be purches in shop for 1350 RP.
