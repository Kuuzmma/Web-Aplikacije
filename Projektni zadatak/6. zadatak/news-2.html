Preseason 2022

Preseason 2022 is right around the corner, and we are excited to introduce with it a brand new Inspiration Keystone: First Strike.

First Strike is a new Inspiration Keystone that gives you a huge advantage when you strike an enemy champion first (or, technically, within a very short window time at the start of champion combat).

Damaging an enemy champion with an attack or ability before they do, or within 0.25s of the start of combat, grants 5 gold and First Strike for 3 seconds, causing your attacks or abilities to deal 12% bonus damage against all champions, and granting (100% melee / 70% Ranged) of bonus damage dealt as gold.

To get the most out of it, youâ€™ll need to beat your opponents in a test of reactions to get that initial hit and follow up to deal as much damage as possible during the 3-second buff.

Cooldown of this keystone is between 25 and 15 secondes based on level of champion. It's designed for players who want more strategic, long-term output from their runes in addition to an immediate mechanical boost.

When one comes, the other goes. We removed first strike prototype Omnistone, which was never been popular.
