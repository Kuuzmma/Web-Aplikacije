What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a team-based game with two teams of five players.

You can choose one of over 140 champions to play and defeat the enemy team.

You can choose one mode of game, but basically most popular is Summoner's rift map. On this map you can choose one of three lanes and one of five position or roles in game. You must have good community in the team and do your best to destroy enemy nexus and win the game.
